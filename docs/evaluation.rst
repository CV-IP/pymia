Evaluation
==========

The modules described in this chapter provide an easy way to evaluate the performance of your algorithms.

We provide an :class:`Evaluator` and two writers (:class:`CSVEvaluatorWriter` and :class:`ConsoleEvaluatorWriter`),
which can be used with a large amount of metrics (see :module:`metric`).
